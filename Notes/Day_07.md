## Boolean Masking

Boolean masking helped filter arrays based on business conditions.

Important points revised:

- Conditions generate Boolean arrays.
- `True` values can be counted using `np.sum()`.
- Multiple conditions require `&`, `|`, and parentheses.
- `np.all()` checks whether every value satisfies a condition.
- `np.any()` checks whether at least one value satisfies a condition.

- Analytical problems often require combining multiple NumPy operations rather than relying on a single function.

- Understanding array shapes and the `axis` parameter is essential for performing accurate calculations.
- Boolean masking is a powerful technique for filtering and analyzing data.
- Broadcasting enables efficient large-scale transformations and business simulations.
- Numerical analysis becomes valuable only when the results are interpreted and translated into meaningful business recommendations.
